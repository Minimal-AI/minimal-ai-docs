title: "CheckoutChamp"
description: "Connect CheckoutChamp to Minimal AI"
---

Connecting CheckoutChamp lets your AI agents look up orders and customer data from your CheckoutChamp funnels.

## What you'll need

- **Username** and **password** — your CheckoutChamp API credentials.

## Find your CheckoutChamp credentials

In CheckoutChamp, open your account settings and go to the API / integrations section to find or create your API username (login ID) and password. If you can't find them, contact CheckoutChamp support.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> CheckoutChamp**, and fill in your username and password.

<Warning>
  Keep these credentials secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's CheckoutChamp integration.
</Warning>
