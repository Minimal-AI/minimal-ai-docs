Connecting Shopware gives your AI agents access to orders, products, and customer data from your Shopware 6 store.

## What you'll need

- **Shop URL** — your store URL, for example `https://my-shop.shopware.store`.
- **Access key ID** (Client ID) and **Secret access key** (Client secret) — from a Shopware integration.
- **Sales channel ID** (optional) — only needed for specific sales-channel setups.

## Create a Shopware integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    In the Shopware 6 Admin, go to **Settings \> System \> Integrations** and click **Add integration**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the integration">
    Give it a name (for example, `Minimal AI`) and the permissions the agent needs. Save. Shopware shows the **Access key ID** and **Secret access key** — copy both.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Shopware**, and fill in your shop URL, Access key ID, and Secret access key. Add a Sales channel ID only if your setup requires it.

<Warning>
  The Secret access key is shown only once. Keep it safe and only paste it into Minimal AI's Shopware integration.
</Warning>
