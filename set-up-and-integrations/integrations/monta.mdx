Connecting Monta lets your AI agents look up fulfillment, stock, and shipment data from your Monta warehouse.

## What you'll need

- **Username** and **password** — your Monta API credentials.

## Find your Monta credentials

Monta provides API credentials (a username and password) for your warehouse account. You can find or request these in MontaPortal under your API/connection settings, or ask your Monta contact.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Monta**, and fill in your username and password.

<Warning>
  Keep these credentials secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's Monta integration.
</Warning>
