---

Connecting Firmhouse lets your AI agents look up subscriptions and customer data from Firmhouse.

## What you'll need

- **Firmhouse token** — a project access token from your Firmhouse project.

## Find your Firmhouse token

In Firmhouse, open your **project settings** and go to the **API / access tokens** section to find your project access token. Use a token with the access level the agent needs.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Firmhouse**, and paste your Firmhouse token.

<Warning>
  Keep your token secret and only paste it into Minimal AI's Firmhouse integration.
</Warning>
