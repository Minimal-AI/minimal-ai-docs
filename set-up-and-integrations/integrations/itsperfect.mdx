Connecting Itsperfect lets your AI agents look up orders, products, and stock from your Itsperfect ERP.

## What you'll need

- **Base URL** — the base URL of your Itsperfect environment/API.
- **Username** and **password** — your Itsperfect API credentials.

## Find your Itsperfect credentials

Itsperfect issues an API base URL and credentials per environment. Open the API settings in your Itsperfect environment, or request the base URL, username, and password from your Itsperfect contact.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Itsperfect**, and fill in your base URL, username, and password.

<Warning>
  Keep these credentials secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's Itsperfect integration.
</Warning>
