---

Connecting ChannelDock lets your AI agents look up orders and stock across the channels you manage in ChannelDock.

## What you'll need

- **API key** and **API secret** — created in your ChannelDock account.

## Find your ChannelDock API credentials

In ChannelDock, open your account **Settings** and go to the **API** section to generate an API key and secret. If you can't find it, contact ChannelDock support.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> ChannelDock**, and fill in your API key and API secret.

<Warning>
  Keep your API key and secret secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's ChannelDock integration.
</Warning>
