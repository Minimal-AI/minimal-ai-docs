title: "ReferralCandy"
description: "Connect ReferralCandy to Minimal AI"
---

Connecting ReferralCandy lets your AI agents look up referral and reward data from ReferralCandy.

## What you'll need

- **Access ID** and **Secret key** — your ReferralCandy API credentials.

## Find your ReferralCandy credentials

In ReferralCandy, open your account settings and go to the **API / integrations** section to find your **Access ID** and **Secret key**. If you can't find them, contact ReferralCandy support.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> ReferralCandy**, and fill in your Access ID and Secret key.

<Warning>
  Keep these credentials secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's ReferralCandy integration.
</Warning>
