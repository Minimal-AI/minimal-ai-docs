---
title: "Other Integrations"
description: "Connect other integrations to Minimal AI"
---

For all other integrations listed on [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations),
retrieve the API key and any other required details from that integration's
account, then paste them into the matching integration in Minimal AI.

If your platform isn't listed, you can still connect it with a
[custom integration](/set-up-and-integrations/integrations/custom-integrations).

For questions, email [niek@gominimal.ai](mailto:niek@gominimal.ai).
