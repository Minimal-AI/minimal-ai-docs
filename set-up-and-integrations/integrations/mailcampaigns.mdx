Connecting MailCampaigns lets your AI agents look up customer and marketing data from MailCampaigns.

## What you'll need

- **Client ID** and **Client secret** — your MailCampaigns API credentials.

## Find your MailCampaigns credentials

In MailCampaigns, open your account settings and go to the **API** section to find or create your Client ID and Client secret. If you can't find them, contact MailCampaigns support.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> MailCampaigns**, and fill in your Client ID and Client secret.

<Warning>
  Keep these credentials secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's MailCampaigns integration.
</Warning>
