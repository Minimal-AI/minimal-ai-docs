---

Connecting Omnidesk lets your AI agents read and reply to conversations in your Omnidesk helpdesk.

## What you'll need

- **Base URL** — your Omnidesk organisation URL, for example `https://org-name.omnidesk.com`.
- **API token** — an API token from your Omnidesk account.

## Find your Omnidesk API token

<Steps>
  <Step title="Note your base URL">
    Your base URL is the address you use to open Omnidesk, in the form `https://org-name.omnidesk.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an API token">
    In Omnidesk, open your account settings and go to the **API** (integrations) section, then generate an API token. If you can't find it, contact your Omnidesk administrator or support.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Omnidesk**, and fill in your base URL and API token.

<Warning>
  Keep your API token secret and only paste it into Minimal AI's Omnidesk integration.
</Warning>
