Connecting Active Ants lets your AI agents look up fulfillment and shipment data from your Active Ants warehouse.

## What you'll need

- **Username** and **password** — your Active Ants API credentials.

## Find your Active Ants credentials

Active Ants issues API credentials per merchant. If you don't have a username and password for the API, request them from your Active Ants contact or account manager.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Active Ants**, and fill in your username and password.

<Warning>
  Keep these credentials secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's Active Ants integration.
</Warning>
