Connecting Yotpo lets your AI agents look up reviews and loyalty data from Yotpo. Which credentials you need depends on which Yotpo products you use.

## What you'll need

**If you use Yotpo Reviews:**

- **App key** and **API secret** — from your Yotpo Reviews settings.

**If you use Yotpo Loyalty & Referrals:**

- **GUID** and **API key** — from your Yotpo Loyalty & Referrals settings.

Provide only the credentials for the products you use.

## Find your Yotpo credentials

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reviews credentials">
    In Yotpo, open your **Reviews** store settings. The **App key** (store ID) and **API secret** (secret key) are shown in the store / API settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Loyalty & Referrals credentials">
    In the Yotpo **Loyalty & Referrals** admin, open the general/API settings to find your **GUID** and **API key**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Yotpo**, and fill in the credentials for the Yotpo products you use.

<Warning>
  Keep your API secret and keys safe and only paste them into Minimal AI's Yotpo integration.
</Warning>
