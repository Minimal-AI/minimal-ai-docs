Connecting QLS lets your AI agents look up shipment and tracking data from QLS.

## What you'll need

- **Email address** and **password** — your QLS login credentials.
- **Company ID** (optional) — only relevant if your QLS account is used for multiple companies.

## Find your QLS details

Use the email address and password you sign in to QLS with. If your account manages multiple companies, ask QLS for your **Company ID**.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> QLS**, and fill in your email address and password. Add a Company ID only if your account is used for multiple companies.

<Warning>
  Keep these credentials secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's QLS integration.
</Warning>
