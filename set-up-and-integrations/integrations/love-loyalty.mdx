description: "Connect Love Loyalty to Minimal AI"
---

Connecting Love Loyalty lets your AI agents look up loyalty and rewards data from Love Loyalty.

## What you'll need

- **API key** — a Love Loyalty API key.

## Find your Love Loyalty API key

In Love Loyalty, open your account settings and go to the **API / integrations** section to find or generate an API key. If you can't find it, contact Love Loyalty support.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Love Loyalty**, and paste your API key.

<Warning>
  Keep your API key secret and only paste it into Minimal AI's Love Loyalty integration.
</Warning>
