Connecting Returnista lets your AI agents look up returns and their status from Returnista.

## What you'll need

- **API token** — a Returnista API token.
- **Account ID** — your Returnista account ID.

## Find your Returnista details

In Returnista, open your account settings and go to the **API / integrations** section to find your API token and account ID. If you can't find them, contact Returnista support.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Returnista**, and fill in your API token and account ID.

<Warning>
  Keep your API token secret and only paste it into Minimal AI's Returnista integration.
</Warning>
