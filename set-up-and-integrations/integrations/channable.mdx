Connecting Channable lets your AI agents work with your Channable feeds and orders.

## What you'll need

- **Channable URL** — the URL of your Channable project/account.
- **Username** and **password** (optional) — only needed if your Channable setup requires them.

## Find your Channable details

Your Channable URL is the address of your Channable project. API access and any required credentials are managed in your Channable account settings under the API / connections section. If you're unsure, contact Channable support.

## Connect in Minimal AI

Go to [Dashboard \> Integrations](https://gominimal.ai/dashboard/integrations), click **Add an integration \> Channable**, and fill in your Channable URL. Add a username and password only if your setup needs them.

<Warning>
  Keep any credentials secret and only paste them into Minimal AI's Channable integration.
</Warning>
