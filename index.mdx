## What is Minimal AI?

Minimal AI lets you set up AI customer support agents that resolve tickets in your
helpdesk just like a human would. You connect your systems, train the agents on
your business processes, and they handle conversations across your channels —
from your helpdesk to your storefront.

Getting the most out of Minimal comes down to three things:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your systems">
    Link your helpdesk, store, and other tools so agents have the context and
    abilities they need. See [Integrations](/set-up-and-integrations/integrations/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and configure an agent">
    Set your agent's channels, stores, tone of voice, and rules. See [AI Agents](/set-up-and-integrations/ai-agents).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train it on your processes">
    Write protocols so the agent follows your business processes. See [Protocols](/training/protocols).
  </Step>
</Steps>
